# Video music / audio licence

The assembled video bed (master + 90s + 30s, `precedent-video-drop/exports/`) uses a
**self-generated low ambient drone** synthesised at assembly time by ffmpeg
(`Prep/video/pipeline/assemble.py`, a filtered sine — no sample, no third-party recording).
**No third-party audio is used, so no external licence is required.**

The voice-over is a scratch track from macOS `say` (offline TTS) over the canonical script
(`Prep/video/pipeline/vo_canonical.json`); the human re-records it per `Prep/video/SHIP-KIT.md`.

If a CC0 / YouTube-Audio-Library music track is swapped in for the final upload, record its
licence URL here before publishing (one line: track · source · licence URL).
